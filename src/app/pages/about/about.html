About Somniation

We turn ambitious ideas into running systems

Our name comes from somnium — Latin for dream. Since 2014 we've been the IT partner for companies that dream bigger than their current infrastructure allows, pairing enterprise-grade engineering with service that still feels personal.

Our mission

Most businesses don't need more technology — they need technology that works. Our mission is to make world-class IT accessible to companies that aren't (yet) enterprises: monitored around the clock, secured by default and planned with a roadmap instead of a sales quota.

Today 60+ specialists support clients across three continents. We're still independent, still engineer-led and still measured on one thing: whether your team's day got easier.
